About


Looking for tips on how to use the map? Click on the tab to learn more.


Open source, GNU licensed, and available on GitHub.

After a couple years of working with a lovely paper map of the same title, the North Start Sierra Club (Minnesota's chapter of the famous organization) decided they wanted to take their project to the web. Through a partnership with the University of St. Thomas (St. Paul, MN) they began work on a map. The first run produced no usable product.

Having improved his web development skills significantly, recent graduate Nick Ronnei headed up a team of newly interested students (Hung Le, Rani Mohanty, and Nate Bettin) and built a better version of the map. This new version is fully responsive thanks to Tobias Bieniek's (https://github.com/Turbo87) wonderful jQuery sidebar!. Nick drew inspiration from David Todd's map Beer Advocate's Top 250 and his custom BeerLeaf template which he forked from Bryan McBride's BootLeaf. A BIG thanks to all of them for their hard work!
